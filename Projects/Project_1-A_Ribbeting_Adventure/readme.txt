Ribbeting_Adventure 


Producer: DeCristo Franceschini 


Programmer: Brendan Rahilly 


Designer: Jacob Welsh 


Artist: Troy Vong Nguyen 


Instructions: Click on the bugs to eat them and increase your jump height, click on platforms to grapple with your tongue. Your hype meter decreases over time, eating bugs refills it. The lower the meter, the lower your jump height. If the meter fully depletes, you lose the game. Make it to the end of the level and eat the final bug to win the game. 


Cheat Codes: 


Alt + G: restarts the room 


Alt + H: restarts the game 


Alt + J: gives the player max hype meter 


Alt + K: reduces the hype meter to 1, losing the game
